Welcome to Share a Bite. Our mission is to preserve your cherished family recipes. We understand that a food recipe is more than a set of instructions or ingredients that simply create a meal. A great family recipe has a story. It has the power to unite loved ones, and become a source of some of the best family memories. So share your family recipe, share its story and let's inspire the next generation to Share A Bite.
